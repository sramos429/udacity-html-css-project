Cat

A kitten's whiskers are as long as their body is wide.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 18 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 13 to 17 years
  • Habitat: On every continent except Antarctica

A typically furry animal that has a long tail and sharp, retractable claws. There are many breeds of cats, but this one in particular is a house cat ready to snuggle, play or eat dinner.
